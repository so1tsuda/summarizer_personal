Gen Z protests swept the world this year, pushing for political change in countries from Turkey and Indonesia to France and Mexico. ⁠
⁠
Demonstrations led by young people were largely driven by anger at political corruption and wealth inequality, and even led to the ousting of government leaders in Madagascar and Nepal.⁠
⁠
Protesters have rallied behind an unlikely symbol: a Jolly Roger-inspired straw hat-wearing pirate flag.
